Overview

Sans the skeleton is a dangerous monster with incredible power. He is, for the most part, passive and lazy. However, if you manage to piss him off, he will not hesitate to give you a very bad time.



Sans is, like all monsters of the underground, susceptible to physical damage. In fact, a cursory scan reveals that Sans has only 1 hp. The only problem is that he is impossible to hit from head on, as he can instantly dodge any incoming attack. The only option is a surprise attack and even that is difficult to pull off.

Termination of Sans


Abilities include:

  • Teleportation/ incredibly fast movement
  • Bone based attacks similar to his brother's, but far more intense
  • Summoning goat skulls that shoot burning lasers
  • "Grounding" a human's soul, again just like his brother
  • Any damage done by Sans gives no "invincibility frames" and leaves behind additional "poison damage"
  • This "poison" cannot kill, but can weaken a human's health down to 1 hp
  • All of these attributes combined makes Sans capable of killing a human in seconds if caught off guard
